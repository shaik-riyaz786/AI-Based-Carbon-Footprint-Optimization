About

The ESG Carbon Footprint Analysis Platform is designed to help organizations measure and optimize their environmental impact through advanced machine learning and AI. By uploading a dataset (CSV or Excel) with or without CO₂e emissions data, users can:

  • Predict CO₂e emissions using a pre-trained XGBoost model.
  • Visualize key drivers of emissions with SHAP feature importance plots.
  • Receive AI-generated ESG reports with actionable sustainability recommendations.
  • Securely manage accounts with user registration and login.

Our mission is to empower businesses to make data-driven decisions for a sustainable future, leveraging cutting-edge AI to analyze carbon footprints and identify ESG risk hotspots.
